Our Story & Philosophy

Combining traditional Indian carpentry craftsmanship with advanced AI design technology.

Crafting Comfort Since 2012

At UNIC Home Furniture, we believe your home is a reflection of your soul. For over a decade, we have been crafting custom premium furniture tailored to Indian households. What started as a boutique wood workshop in Andhra Pradesh has now transformed into a modern luxury furniture-tech experience.

We hand-select high-durability hardwoods like Teak and Sheesham, combining them with high-density padding and premium upholstery. Every L-type sofa, recliner, cot, and chair is designed to last a lifetime, ensuring your investment is one of comfort, beauty, and absolute quality.

Today, we are bridging the gap between traditional craftsmanship and digital convenience. By introducing our AI Room Match assistant, we allow customers to photograph their spaces and automatically find custom-colored furniture sets that integrate seamlessly with their wall paint and overall lighting.

The UNIC Pillars

We build our brand and products on three unwavering principles.

📐

Ergonomic Luxury

All furniture is structurally planned to reduce body strain, using calibrated angles, pocket-spring comfort layers, and adjustable backrests.

🪵

Sustainable Timber

We source our solid woods ethically from licensed plantation forests. Teak and Indian Rosewood (Sheesham) form the core structural frame of our cots and sofas.
