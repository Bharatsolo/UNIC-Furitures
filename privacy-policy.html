Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to UNIC Home Furniture. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a secure experience when browsing our collections or using our digital services. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, store, and process your information when you fill out contact forms or use our room-matching tools.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect information that is strictly necessary to fulfill your furniture inquiries or provide AI services:

  • Inquiry Details: Name, phone number, email address, message, and specific category selections.
  • Room Images: If you upload images of your room using the Match My Room tool, we process these images locally in your browser or securely transmit them to the Gemini API solely for color extraction and matching.
  • Automatic Logs: Basic device parameters and traffic metrics to keep the site fast and reliable.

3. Image Processing Policy

Your uploaded room photos are not stored permanently on our servers. The color-palette extraction occurs client-side and via secure Gemini API endpoints. Once you close your browser tab or complete the analysis session, your uploaded images are discarded from memory.

4. How We Use Your Data

We use your details to formulate direct quotes, answer questions via WhatsApp, arrange showroom appointments, and coordinate final product deliveries. We never sell, lease, or distribute your email or contact phone details to any third-party marketing companies.

5. Contacting Us

If you have questions about our privacy policy or wish to review details you sent us, please email our support team at srikrishnaUnicMobiles@gmail.com.
